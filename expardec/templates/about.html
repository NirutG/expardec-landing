About Us

EXPARDEC is a social enterprise based in southern Ecuador that seeks to transform shrimp aquaculture into a more just, ecological, and human-centered practice. Our name stands for Expanding partnerships for dignity, ecological balance, and circular economy.

We aim to build a model that empowers artisanal farmers, protects ecosystems, and promotes a fairer and more resilient blue economy.

At EXPARDEC, we believe the future of aquaculture is not about producing more, but about producing better. Through fair trade, value addition, and capacity building, we strive to elevate artisanal aquaculture as a premium and sustainable alternative.

Our goal is to promote a system that avoids oversaturation, supports price stability, and protects both livelihoods and coastal ecosystems.

We are not here to compete for volume, we’re here to grow with purpose, dignity, and impact.
